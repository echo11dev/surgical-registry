Complications Dataset
Standardized Hip & Knee Society definitions • Ready for publications & benchmarking

Comprehensive one-row-per-surgery export designed specifically for our research division and future collaborative studies.

Key Features:
  • Surgery metadata + standardized procedure names
  • Elixhauser Comorbidity Score (van Walraven)
  • Research project enrollment (multiple per case)
  • Key complications expanded with Yes/No + Date columns
  • Full complications JSON for custom parsing
  • Implant count and outpatient status
Includes complications aligned with The Knee Society (TKA 2013) and The Hip Society (THA 2015) standardized lists.

Download Complications Dataset CSV
Ideal for time-to-event analysis, risk stratification, and grant-supported research
Usage Notes
  • All exports are de-identified where appropriate for research use.
  • Procedure names follow our standardized algorithm (e.g., "Left Total Knee Arthroplasty").
  • Date fields enable calculation of time-to-complication metrics.
  • Complications stored as structured JSON for future severity grading (Grade 1–5) and advanced queries.
